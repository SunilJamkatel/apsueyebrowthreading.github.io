“What is facial threading?” Lots of people have this question. Threading is an ancient method of unwanted hair removal from face. Artist use unique technique by cotton thread is twisted and rolled to pull out unwanted hair from person’s face. It is very precise than waxing and gives clients much better shapes. Facial threading includes eyebrows, upper lip, lower lip, forehead, chin, sides, cheeks, mid brow, neck or full face.
